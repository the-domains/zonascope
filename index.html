ZONASCOPE: Get in The Zone

Now there is a way to track and determine when you are going to most productive in a day. ZONASCOPE is a new app for iPhone that lets you play brain training games and then uses those scores to build a personal productivity profile of when you are most during the day. ZONASCOPE works out the best time to get you into "THE ZONE" of optimal performance.
